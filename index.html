Hello there! I’m glad you found your way to my website!

This is a curation of all the stuff I’ve been working on over the last few years. My projects have spanned across art, design, graphics, writing, innovation and research. As a major in Public Space Design, I’m interested in studying and documenting socio-spatial dynamics and the way they shape neighbourhoods. Working at urbz has been a step in this direction and has helped nuance my understanding of the same. I’m generally a curious person who loves to know how different things work. I’m also a contemplater, a traveller, a reader, a knitter, a planner and a strategist!

If you’re interested in working together, write to me at hello@radha.design

Or follow me on Dribbble , Behance , Linkedin and Instagram to stay updated with my latest work!
